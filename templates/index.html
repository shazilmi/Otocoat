Otocoat:

Empowering Rajagiri Faculty with Effortless Course Outcome Assessment!

Otocoat is an online tool designed to calculate the Course Outcome Attainment Score in a manner that is easy, quick, and efficient. This tool is exclusively available for use by the faculty of Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology. With Otocoat, faculty members can conveniently assess and determine the level of achievement of course outcomes, streamlining the evaluation process and enabling them to gauge the effectiveness of their teaching methodologies. AN OVERVIEW OF OUR PROJECT

Calculation of Course Outcome Attainment score is currently a cumbersome process, and our project aims to make it a simple and quick one. Once a teacher logs in to the website using their Rajagiri email id, they can choose the class and the evaluation method for which marks are to be entered. The details of the students of that class will be made available along with the current marks, if entered. Once the marks are input by the teacher, the course outcome attainment score is calculated and displayed. Total course outcome calculation is also available to display.
