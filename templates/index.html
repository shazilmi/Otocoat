Otocoat

Otocoat is an online tool for calculation of Course Outcome Attainment Score in a way that is easy, quick and efficient.
This is available for use exclusively for Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology faculty.

AN OVERVIEW OF OUR PROJECT

Calculation of Course Outcome Attainment score is currently a cumbersome process, and our project aims to make it a simple and quick one. Once a teacher logs in to the website using their Rajagiri email id, they can choose the class and the evaluation method for which marks are to be entered. The details of the students of that class will be made available along with the current marks, if entered. Once the marks are input by the teacher, the course outcome attainment score is calculated and displayed. Total course outcome calculation is also available to display.
